## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Browse the jewelry collection
3. Click "Add to Cart" on any product
4. View your cart by clicking the cart icon in the header
5. Adjust quantities or remove items from the cart
6. Click "Checkout" to complete your purchase (demo mode)
